e-plantShopping
Summary of Project Requirements:
Landing Page: Company name, background image, "About Us" paragraph, and a "Get Started" button (links to Product Listing).

Product Listing Page:

Header: Logo/Name (links back home), tagline, and a Cart Icon with a dynamic item counter.

Categories: Plants grouped by features (e.g., Air Purifying, Aromatic).

Plant Cards: Image, Name, Price, Description, and an "Add to Cart" button.

Button Logic: "Add to Cart" must change to "Added to Cart" and be disabled once clicked.

Shopping Cart Page:

Item Management: Cards showing thumbnail, unit price, and subtotal.

Quantity Controls: Increment, decrement, and delete buttons.

Global Totals: Total number of plants and total cost for all items.

Logic Sync: If an item quantity reaches zero or is deleted, the "Add to Cart" button on the product page must be re-enabled.
